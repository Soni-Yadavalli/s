FOR YOU❤


I don't love you because you are perfect,
or because you say the right things,
or because you make the world little easier to bear.
I LOVE YOU because my heart chose you quitely without any reason.
It's not something I planned,
not something I could stop even If i tried,
It just chose you.
I love you when you smile,
But also when you don't.
I love you in silence,in distance, in spaces where the woods can't reach.
You ask me "WHY ME?"
And I could list thousand small things,
but none of them would be true because,
LOVE doesnot need any reason,
It just is!
So if you ever doubt it, remeber this:
I may not know how much I LOVE YOU
But I know I LOVE YOU beyond anything,
Beyond any reason,
Beyond time,
Beyond even MYSELF!
